# Agentic Economy Track

**Build autonomous agents that transact on Arc.**

AI agents that hold wallets, make payments, manage risk, and settle in USDC without human intervention—whether that's nano payments between services, autonomous treasury management, or agent-to-agent commerce.

## What we're looking for:
- Agents with clear decision logic tied to real signals
- Autonomous spending, payments, or settlement flows
- Use of Nanopayments for micro-transactions between agents or services
- USDC-denominated operations with demonstrable autonomy

## Tools
Arc, USDC, Circle Wallets, Circle Contracts, Nanopayments, Paymaster
